MyStylist - Virutal Wardrobe

What is MyStylist?

MyStlist is the virtual equivallent of your wardrobe and clothing! Countless times we have gotten in the situation of not knowing what we have in our wardrobe, what we need to buy, what we like, what out style is... Now, with MyStylist, all of those problems are no more!

Add to Wardrobe!

MyStlist allows you to add whatever you have in your wardrobe to your online repository. Siply add the clothes you have, based on their category, style and your preference! MyStylist keeps track of the times you wear the clothes, and gives you insight on your favourite items!

Create your Styles!

With your added items, you can set them into Styles and Outfits. Adding a style works just like adding clothes. All your outfits are kept in a repository, and when you need an outfit, just open the app. You can also log outfits that you wear, keeping insight to what your favourite style is!

See you Stats!

Based of the stuff you wear, MyStylist keeps count of all the items you wear the most! Using this data, MyStlist tells you what your style is. Use this data to change your personal Image, or keep it even more the same, whatever your creative mind imagines!
